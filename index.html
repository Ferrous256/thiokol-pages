Featured System · Main Speakers

Void Acoustics
Tri-Motion

Void Acoustics has become a globally recognized name in professional audio — their signature systems are sought after by iconic venue owners and event organizers worldwide, celebrated for an extraordinary combination of sonic performance and distinctive visual design. The Tri-Motion is Void's flagship full-range speaker: three transducers, each loaded by an isometric conical horn, delivering an exceptionally balanced sound field with stunning definition across the full frequency range.

Where most speakers are designed to be unnoticed, a Void system is designed to enhance the atmosphere of a venue — acoustically and visually. Two Tri-Motions form the core of our rig.

Subwoofers

JTR Orbit Shifter Pro × 2

These are serious subwoofers — built on a no-compromises philosophy, each unit houses a 9-foot folded horn loaded by an 18" driver engineered for maximum low-frequency output with minimal distortion. At 185 lbs each, they are purpose-built for professional deployment. The result is bass that you feel as much as hear — clean, authoritative, and capable of filling outdoor spaces that would swallow lesser systems.
